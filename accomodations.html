Accomodation

The individual cottages are set among the palms that line the beach frontage. Each has a veranda overlooking the sea and a private secluded sunbathing area complete with rustic parasol. The rooms themselves are bright and breezy with a huge billowing mosquito net and plenty of windows allowing good airflow and natural light.

They have spacious en-suite bathrooms, with a large walk-in shower and hot water heated by solar power.

All beach cottages have the following facilities:

  • Large en-suite shower room with solar heated hot water
  • King Size Bed (covered by a mosquito net). This can be split into two single beds
  • 100% cotton bed linen
  • Overhead fans
  • Bath and Beach Towels
  • Covered Veranda
  • Private sunbathing area on beach in front of cottage
